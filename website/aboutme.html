How I Ended Up Here? or From Science to ACTING

I was not always passionate about acting. In high school, I was more of a science nerd, completely immersed in textbooks and experiments.

I even worked on projects related to satellites, focusing on reducing the amount of space debris and trash in space. While my classmates were exploring creative outlets, I was busy solving equations and trying to make space a cleaner place.

At the time, the world of performing arts seemed distant, but it did not take long for me to realize that there is more to life than just science. It was through exploring different interests that I found my true passion in acting a path that has allowed me to express myself in ways I never thought possible.
